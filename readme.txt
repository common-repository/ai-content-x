== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= What is AI Content X? =

AI Content X is a powerful WordPress plugin that utilizes artificial intelligence and natural language processing techniques to generate high-quality, engaging content for your website or blog. With AI Content X, you can input your ideas or outlines and let the AI do the rest, saving you time and effort on content creation while still producing top-quality content for your audience.

= How does AI Content X work? =

To use AI Content X, simply input a prompt outlining the content you want the plugin to generate and let the AI do the rest. You can then review the generated content and make any necessary edits or adjustments before publishing it on your website or blog. You can also use the plugin's cron job feature to set it to generate and publish new content on a regular basis, ensuring that your website or blog always has fresh, up-to-date content for your readers.
